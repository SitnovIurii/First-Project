Setup and thoughts

Let's talk about my build. It's:

  • Rig: Fractal Design Meshify 2
  • Processor: AMD Ryzen 9 7950X
  • Motherboard: MSI X670E ACE
  • RAM: G.Skill Trident Z5 6000 2x16Gb
  • ROM: Samsung 990 PRO
  • Cooling: NZXT Kraken X73 360
  • GPU: MSI RTX 4080 Suprim X
  • PSU: Enermax Platimax 750W

First of all - rig, mid tower with possibility to put there an E-ATX motherboard, well ventilated and has 3 antidust meshes. Next platform I've decided on am5 due to freshness and possibility to upgrade in future to newest CPU in the end of platform lifetime. I choosed a R9 7950X because of high boost clocks and multithread performance as well as comparly low wattage draw. Next the most hard part on deciding of motherboard.

Friend's requirements and wishes

My friend had using an Intel i7 8700K, but due to circut on motherboard or cpu itself. Thus, he had to buy new setup and he asked me to help with choise.
First of all we argue about what kind of programms he used to execute. The answer was games, but at the same time he was utilizing cpu power at Solid work. Thus we decided on Intel cpu due to more core count and relative price. Next, while Intel cpus are near to limit of it's overclocking and powerdraw even on i5 is above 200W at boost state, we decided to save money and buy a B760 chipset. Here I have decided to buy an Asus Strix motherboard due to it's optimal power stages to withstand with i5 13600k. And finally we bought a Corsair DDR5 6000MHz memory because novadays we have some limits if we using a DDR4 memory in heavy workload scenario.
